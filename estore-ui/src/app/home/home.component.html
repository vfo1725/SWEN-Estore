Mission Statement

With fair deals and frequent sales, Music Store ROC is a music store specializing in the sale of vinyls, cds, and music playing devices. We are family owned, and have served the Rochester area for 15 years. We created this website to not only connect further with our loyal customers here in Rochester, but to expand our services to everyone across the country.
